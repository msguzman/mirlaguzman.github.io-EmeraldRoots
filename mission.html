Our Mission

At Emerald Roots, our mission is to restore harmony between Ireland’s landscapes, communities, and food systems through regenerative agroforestry. We are committed to integrating trees, sustainable farming practices, and biodiversity-friendly approaches that enrich the soil, support wildlife, and empower local growers. By blending traditional Irish land stewardship with modern ecological innovation, we aim to cultivate resilient farms, healthy ecosystems, and a greener future for generations to come.

Projects

Our projects are designed to demonstrate the benefits of agroforestry and regenerative land management in Ireland. Through collaborative efforts, research, and hands-on implementation, we aim to create sustainable models that can be replicated across the country. Below are some of our key initiatives:

Erasmus

Our projects focus on learning, innovation, and hands-on ecological restoration. Through our Erasmus partnerships, we collaborate with European organisations to exchange knowledge, train young people, and develop forward-thinking approaches to sustainable land use. Alongside this, our Agroforestry Test Sites across Ireland allow us to trial diverse tree–crop–livestock systems, monitor soil and biodiversity improvements, and demonstrate practical solutions for resilient farming. Together, these projects help us build a strong, science-based foundation for regenerative land management and community education.

Agroforestry Test Sites

Our agroforestry test sites across Ireland act as living demonstrations of how trees, farming, and biodiversity can work together. Each site trials different Irish-native species, soil conditions, and farm systems, allowing us to monitor improvements in soil health, water retention, wildlife habitat, and farm resilience. These real-world test areas provide essential data and hands-on learning opportunities that guide the future of regenerative agriculture in Ireland.
